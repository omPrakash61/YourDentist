About Me



Dr.Roushan Chaturvedi hates pain. He doesn’t like to suffer
from pain and dental anxiety and will make sure that you
don’t either. On all seven days of the week, you will find
him enthusiastically treating patients at his clinic. He is a
complete family dentist, and you may visit him to care
for your teeth as well as those of your children and older
adults.

       He is passionate about dentistry and strives
to provide high-quality dental care for all types of dental
needs under one roof. His clinic is called
DentistationTM - a name he has trademarked for his practice. He feels communication and education are critical aspects of dental treatment which are often ignored. He enjoys explaining the treatment aspects of dental problems to patients before beginning any procedure. Pain is his number one enemy and he will try his best to prevent it during his work on your teeth. His patients most often remark about his efficient and empathetic nature. He enjoys providing holistic and well-rounded dental care for his patients.

        He has more than 5 years of extensive clinical experience in dentistry so you can be sure you are in safe hands by trusting your dental care to him. He completed his Bachelor’s and Master’s from Christian Dental College, Ludhiana in 2007 and went on to pursue a fellowship in Implantology in 2018. He has published extensively in national and international journals and has over 200 citations to his credit. He is a registered member of the Dental Council of India and the Indian Dental Association, Association of Oral Implantologists, and Indian Society of Pedodontic and Preventive Dentistry among others. He is also a Fellow of the Academy of Oral Implantologists (AOI) and the Academy of General Education (FAGE), Manipal.

         Dr. XYZ has always enjoyed teaching and after a long career teaching young graduate and postgraduate students at Subharti Dental College and Hospital, he decided to extend his teaching skills at M. M. Hospital, Hapur. He is a course coordinator and key educator for Dental Certificate Workshops for young dentists where he frequently conducts Continuing Dental Education Courses all round the year as well.

In his spare time, He enjoys playing cricket, watching Netflix, and attending dental conferences to update his knowledge all over India. He is also the doting father of a daughter who takes up most of his heart.
